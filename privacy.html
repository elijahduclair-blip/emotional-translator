Emotional Translator Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026.

ChatGPT tool requests

The read-only ChatGPT Action sends the word, phrase, or concept needed for a translation or context lookup to the Emotional Translator API. Requests may be processed by the hosting provider's operational logs.

What the tool does not access

The ChatGPT Action does not expose account credentials, authentication tokens, personal profiles, research drafts, approval records, or graph administration. It cannot create, edit, approve, or delete shared graph information.

Data use

Request text is used to return approved color-climate translations and compact graph context. The application does not sell personal data.

Boundaries

Outputs are interpretive relational vocabulary, not medical diagnosis, proof of identity, or universal truth.

Contact

Questions can be submitted through the project's public GitHub repository.
